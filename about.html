I bring you this portfolio showcasing five years of User Experience learning and design. My journey began at NC State University, where I became heavily involved in LACElab, under the mentorship of Dr. Anne McLaughlin. It was there that I discovered and developed my passion for psychology and technology. During my time at LACElab, I participated in undergraduate research and UX projects, even traveling to the Johnson Space Center for a NASA-funded initiative. I thoroughly enjoyed my time there, earning a promotion to Lab Manager during my senior year, which provided a solid foundation for my professional career.

Following my graduation, I completed an internship at Lenovo, which paved the way for a full-time position as an Associate UX Designer for five years. Unfortunately, my position was affected by a company-wide layoff, marking the end of an incredible chapter in my career. However, I view this transition as an opportunity to leverage my skills and experiences to explore new challenges and contribute to innovative projects.

I love the outdoors, including fishing and camping. I have a passion for animal care, particularly for my two Devon Rex kittens, and I find great joy in reading fantasy literature. Additionally, I am an avid video gamer, playing RPGs, FPS, and Management Colony style games.
