This portfolio is a built-from-scratch website showcasing five years of User Experience learning and design. My journey began at NC State University, where I became heavily involved in LACElab, under the mentorship of Dr. Anne McLaughlin.
It was there that I discovered and developed my passion for psychology and technology. During my time at LACElab, I participated in undergraduate research and UX projects, even traveling to the Johnson Space Center for a NASA-funded initiative to interview
engineers and scientists. I thoroughly enjoyed my time there, earning a promotion to Lab Manager during my senior year, which provided a solid foundation for my professional career.

Following my graduation, I completed an internship at Lenovo, which paved the way for a full-time position as an Associate UX Designer for five years.
Unfortunately, my position was affected by a company-wide layoff, marking the end of an incredible chapter in my career.
However, I view this transition as an opportunity to leverage my skills and experiences to explore new challenges and contribute to innovative projects while in the meantime learning HTML and CSS.
